System Engine Diagnostics & FAQ
Q: Is this engine completely optimized for high-yield books like Narendra Awasthi or Allen Modules?
A: Yes. The parser isolates core scientific terminology and maps phrases to the precise Hindi equivalents required for competitive medical exams while preserving chemical structures perfectly.
Q: Why are equations, values, and units like kJ/mol or atm left untranslated?
A: National Testing Agency (NTA) standards dictate that standard parameters, equations, and numeric notations must maintain universal structural representations to prevent analytical interpretation errors.
Q: Why did an image upload or scanned book fail to show accurate output lines?
A: This localized client-side browser pipeline requires native vector text layers. Pure picture-scanned files require server-side OCR arrays and will generate layout warnings.
