How does the "Same to Same" layout mode preserve N. Avasthi double columns?
Instead of extracting paragraphs into a separate list, this layout system fetches the precise pixel bounding box of every line. It white-masks the English characters and puts Hindi text on top at the exact same location.
Are numbers, formulas, and scientific fractions preserved?
Yes, numbers, math symbols (+, -, =, /), variables (P, V, T, n, K), brackets, and formulas (H2O, CO2) are dynamically bypassed to prevent broken equations.
